The Life of the Prophet

By Ahmed Rizvi

$12.99

A book with this title usually refers to the Seerah — the biography of Prophet Muhammadﷺ. It covers his birth in Makkah, his early life, the first revelation, the message of Islam, struggles in Makkah, the migration to Madinah, major events like Badr and Uhud, establishment of the Muslim community, character traits, teachings, and his final sermon. Seerah books aim to show his moral example, leadership, compassion, and spiritual mission.
