Festivals and its celebration in islam

By Laila Noor

$14.00

Books on Islamic festivals describe the two major celebrations of the Muslim world: Eid al Fitr and Eid al-Adha. They explain how Eid al-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan and is celebrated with communal prayers, charity, and feasts, while Eid al-Adha commemorates Prophet Ibrahim’s willingness to sacrifice his son and includes the ritual of Qurbani along with global Hajj traditions.
