Stories from Quran

By S. Khan

$9.50

These books focus on narrative passages found in the Qur’an. They include stories of prophets like Adam, Nuh (Noah), Ibrahim, Musa (Moses), Isa (Jesus), and many others. The stories highlight themes of faith, patience, justice, divine help, morality, and consequences of human behavior. Unlike storytelling for entertainment, Qur’anic stories are meant to teach lessons and spiritual wisdom.
