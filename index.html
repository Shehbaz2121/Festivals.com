ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This project is dedicated to celebrating the beauty, history, and depth of the world’s religious festivals. Each tradition carries a story of devotion, resilience, memory, and community—woven through centuries of culture and spiritual expression.

Through historical insights, visual storytelling, and carefully curated narratives, we explore the festivals that shape identities and unite communities across the globe.
